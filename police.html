Introduction

Welcome to Project Kusanagi, an action-packed 2D cyber-ninja platformer game built for Android devices. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if you decide to use our game.

By downloading, installing, or playing Project Kusanagi, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

Information Collection

Project Kusanagi is designed to protect your privacy. We prioritize data minimization and transparency.

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location).
  • Local Storage: The game stores your unlocked level progress (up to Level 50), high scores, sound preferences, and kunai inventory locally on your device via secure Shared Preferences. This data stays on your device.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal data, your information is only processed locally on your device to:

  • Maintain your current game progress, levels unlocked, and achievements.
  • Save your game configurations, such as music and SFX settings.
  • Provide a responsive, high-performance gameplay experience.

Third-Party Services

Our game may utilize core Google Play features to run. These services may collect information used to identify your device for functional stability:

  • Google Play Services: For standard binary distribution, licensing, and stability reports.

Please refer to the Google Privacy Policy for more information on how Google manages analytics and device indicators.

Security

We value your trust in playing our game. Because your progress is stored locally on your device, the security of your game progress depends on the security settings of your Android device. We advise keeping your operating system updated and securing your device with PIN/biometrics.

Children's Privacy

Project Kusanagi does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, children's privacy is fully protected in compliance with COPPA.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
